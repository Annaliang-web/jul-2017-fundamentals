Chocolate Chip Recipe

This is my gramma's cookie recipe.

Ingredients

  • sugar
  • flour
  • chocolate chips
  • eggs
  • baking powder
  • butter

Instructions

  1. mix ingredients
  2. roll balls of cookie dough
  3. place onto a baking sheet
  4. bake in oven for 20 minutes at 375°F
